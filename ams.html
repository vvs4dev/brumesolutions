Azure Managed Service

An Azure managed IT service is an Information technology task provided by third party (Brume) and delivered to customer. In Managed service.

  1. Provider retains the responsibility for the functionality of IT service and equipment and for that customer pays a monthly fee

  2. Transfer the burden of maintaining IT(Servers, application, SIAM) from customer to Provider

  3. Customer benefits from predictable pricing and the ability to focus on core business concerns than IT management cores



Benefits

Focus on core business functions

  1. Utilize resources for strategic initiatives not operational

Obtain Enterprise partner expertise

  1. Continuous improvement through standardization, simplification, automation & innovation

  2. Benefit from reduced cost, improved service quality and agility

Reduced TCO

  1. Benefit from economies scale & achieve cost predictability

Risk Mitigation

  1. Handing over IT to provider(Brume) will benefit customer in mitigating risk of leaving any vulnerability

  2. Provider will ensure this by providing intact process of organizational best practices



Why Customer should go for Managed Cloud

Challenges

  1. Seeking optimize & manage cloud infrastructure resources

  2. Compliant with organizational security best practices

  3. Burden of maintaining and managing servers, desktops & application for various hardware & application Issue

  4. Lack of SIAM process(Service integration & Management)- Part of ITSM for change request & deployment strategies of workloads

Solutions/Approach

  1. With Brume’s established Cloud team and being Gold partner of Microsoft, it helps to optimize, monitor and manage customer’s Azure environment from their virtual machines, system, databases, IT security e.t.c

  2. Brume manage everything from uploading customer’s application in Azure to deployment which includes provisioning, monitoring, and load balancing for on-going availability of services.



Managed Cloud Services

With Azure Managed service Customer can

  1. Add or remove cloud infrastructure components

  2. Configure and make changes in settings of cloud infrastructure components

  3. Leverage monitoring, alerts and audit logs data

  4. Troubleshoot and support provisioned cloud infrastructure

  5. Contact to cloud provider on behalf of customer

  6. Monitoring the services installed for their functioning

  7. Backup & restore support

List of service which Azure provide as Managed service

  1. Security & Compliance

  2. Backups & Archives

  3. Disaster Recovery

  4. Networks

  5. Active directory service

  6. SQL Database configuration & management

  7. SIAM process
